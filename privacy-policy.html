Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Simple Rabbit LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates simplerabbit.studio. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Submit a contact form (name, email, phone, message)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (email address)
  • Reach out via email or phone

We also collect non-personal data automatically through Google Analytics, including pages visited, time on site, device type, and general geographic location (city/region level). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry or project interest
  • To send our newsletter if you've subscribed (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • To follow up on active client projects

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party tools to operate this site:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — contact form processing. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Flodesk — email newsletter delivery. Flodesk Privacy Policy

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review them.

Cookies

This site uses cookies through Google Analytics to track aggregate usage patterns. No personal data is stored in these cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to use this site.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for reasonable business records. Newsletter subscribers' email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time via the link in any email
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the change. Continued use of this site after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
