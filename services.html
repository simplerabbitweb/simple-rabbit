The process

Simple. Intentional. No surprises.

Most projects are complete in 4–6 weeks from kickoff. Here's exactly how it goes.

01

Discovery call

A 30-minute conversation. We learn your business, your clients, and what's not working. You learn our process. We decide together if it's the right fit.

02

Proposal & agreement

We send a detailed proposal outlining scope, timeline, and investment. Once you're ready to move forward, we sign the agreement and get started.

03

Questionnaire & kick-off

You complete a focused questionnaire about your business, clients, and goals. Then we meet for a 45-minute kick-off call to align on direction before anything is built.

04

Strategy & copy

We map the site structure and sharpen your messaging before a single page is designed. The words come first. The design serves them.

05

Design & build

We design, build, and optimize your full site. You review at key milestones. Revisions are part of the process, not a negotiation.

06

Launch & support

We launch, submit to Google, and stay available as your partner. When your business changes, your site should change with it.
